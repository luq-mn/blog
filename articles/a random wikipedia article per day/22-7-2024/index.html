Hardie Scott

A very random article indeed. 22nd July 2024, GMT+8 Kuala Lumpur

Who is this guy

(7th June 1907 - 2nd November 1999) He was a Republican member of the United States House of Representatives from Pennsylvania. Born in Bala Cynwyd, Pennsylvania, he was the son of John Roger Kirkpatrick Scott - who was also a Republican member of the House of Representatives from Pennsylvania. He was a graduate of the Taft School in Watertown, Connecticut (1926), Yale University (1930), and the University of Pennsylvania Law School (1934). While in Yale, he was the captain of the 1930 Polo team. That year, the team went on to win the intercollege polo championship.

Political career

Scott was elected as a Republican to the Eightieth, Eighty-first and Eighty-second Congresses. In Congress, Scott introduced the bill that authorized the creation of what eventually became Independece National Historical Park.
